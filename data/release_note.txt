RELEASE NOTE -- Stealth Company v3.1
Date: 2026-05-12

Summary: AI Chat Assistant Launch

Effective May 12, an AI chatbot is live on the website and client portal. The chatbot handles tier-1 support queries autonomously including password resets, billing FAQs, and plan information.

Key operational changes:
1. Unresolved chat queries are automatically escalated to Zendesk as new tickets tagged [chat-escalation]. Full conversation history is attached to every chat-escalated ticket.
2. Average inbound ticket volume to the CX team is expected to drop 35%.
3. SLA clock for chat-escalated tickets starts at the moment the chatbot fails to resolve the query, not when the agent picks it up.
4. CX agents are now responsible for reviewing chatbot responses on escalated tickets as part of the QA process. A new QA checklist item must be added: "Was the chatbot response accurate before escalation?"
5. Weekly reporting metrics must be rebaselined. Ticket volume, first response time, and resolution rate will all be affected by the 35% volume drop. Historical comparisons must note the chatbot launch date as a baseline shift.
