# Weekly CX Reporting SOP
Version: 1.8
Last updated: 2026-03-10

## Purpose
To produce a consistent weekly CX performance report for the CX Lead and VP of Customer Success.

## Target Team
CX Operations

## Related Teams
CX Support Agents, Business Intelligence, Executive Team

## Steps

### Step 1 -- Data Pull
Every Monday at 9am, export the following from Zendesk:
- Total tickets created (week)
- Total tickets resolved (week)
- First response time (average by priority)
- Resolution time (average by priority)
- CSAT score (average)
- SLA compliance rate (by priority)

### Step 2 -- Report Assembly
Populate the weekly CX report template (Google Sheets: CX Ops > Weekly Reports). Compare current week metrics to the previous week and the 4-week rolling average.

### Step 3 -- Trend Analysis
Flag any metric that has moved more than 10% week-over-week. Add a one-line explanation for each flagged metric.

### Step 4 -- Distribution
Share the completed report in #cx-leadership by 11am every Monday. Tag the CX Lead and VP of Customer Success.

### Step 5 -- Archiving
Save the completed report in the Google Drive archive (CX Ops > Reports > Weekly > 2026).
