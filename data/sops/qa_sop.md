# QA Monitoring SOP
Version: 1.5
Last updated: 2026-01-28

## Purpose
To ensure consistent quality of agent responses across all support tickets through regular QA reviews.

## Target Team
CX Operations, Senior CX Specialists

## Related Teams
CX Support Agents, People Operations

## Steps

### Step 1 -- QA Sample Selection
Every week, randomly select 10% of resolved tickets from the previous week for QA review. Use Zendesk's random ticket selector or manually select from the resolved queue.

### Step 2 -- QA Scorecard
Review each selected ticket against the QA scorecard (Notion: CX Ops > QA Scorecard). Score the following items:
- Tone and professionalism (1-5)
- Accuracy of information provided (1-5)
- Resolution speed relative to SLA (1-5)
- Proper escalation if needed (1-5)

### Step 3 -- Agent Feedback
Share QA scores with each agent individually via a private Slack message. Highlight one strength and one area for improvement per ticket reviewed.

### Step 4 -- Team Reporting
Compile QA scores into the weekly CX Ops report. Flag any agent scoring below 3.0 average for coaching review.

### Step 5 -- Coaching
Agents scoring below 3.0 for two consecutive weeks are enrolled in a 2-week coaching plan with their team lead.
