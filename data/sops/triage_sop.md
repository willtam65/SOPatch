# Inbound Ticket Triage SOP
Version: 1.2
Last updated: 2026-01-15

## Purpose
To ensure all inbound support requests are triaged consistently and responded to within SLA windows.

## Target Team
CX Support Agents

## Related Teams
CX Operations, Product, Engineering

## Steps

### Step 1 -- Inbound Ticket Receipt
All inbound support requests arrive via the support email (support@stealthco.com) or the Submit a Request form in the client portal. Monitor the Zendesk queue every 30 minutes during business hours.

### Step 2 -- Priority Assignment
Review the ticket content and assign a priority level:
- P1: System outage, data loss, or complete service unavailability
- P2: Major feature not working, significant business impact
- P3: Minor issue, cosmetic bug, general question

### Step 3 -- Initial Response
Respond to the client within the SLA window based on priority:
- P1: 1 hour
- P2: 4 hours
- P3: 8 hours

Request full context from the client before proceeding if the issue is unclear.

### Step 4 -- Assignment
Assign the ticket to the relevant specialist based on ticket category (Billing, Technical, Account).

### Step 5 -- Escalation
If the ticket cannot be resolved at agent level, escalate to the Senior CX Specialist via Slack #escalations.
